Features

Of

Bhaktapur

Scroll Down To View Content

It is the home of traditional art and architecture, historical monuments and craft work, magnificent windows, pottery and weaving industries, excellent temples, beautiful ponds, rich local customs, culture, religion, festivals, musical mystic and so on. Bhaktapur is still an untouched as well as preserved ancient city that is itself the world to explore for tourists. Here are so many heritage sites to view Nepal's ancient history. From time immemorial it lay on he trade route between Tibet and India. This position on the main caravan route made the town rich and prosperous.

The male inhabitants of this city wear a special type of cap called the Bhadgaule Topi with Daura Suruwal whereas the female wear Hakupatasi and Whantang Suruwal. These are the cultural dresses of Newar community that they have been wearing from thier ancient times. Newar community people were recognized from their dress and their language.
